# Malaysia Aviation & Economic Insights

**A sophisticated exploration of aviation's role in Malaysia's economic growth and global connectivity.**

![Private Jet Flying Over KL Skyline](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1570710891163-6d3b5c47248b?ixlib=rb-4.0.3&auto=format&fit=crop&q=80&w=1920)

This site delivers data-driven analysis, policy insights, and forward-looking perspectives on Malaysia's aviation sector — particularly during the landmark **Visit Malaysia Year 2026**.

### Key Features
- **Elegant Design** — Clean, luxurious interface inspired by high-end conglomerate aesthetics (navy, gold, glassmorphism)
- **Dynamic Elements** — Subtle animations including a moving private jet and morphing background
- **Navigation**
  - [Home](https://mariameram.github.io) — Overview and welcome
  - [Blog Posts](/blog.html) — In-depth articles and commentary
  - [Data Visuals](/data.html) — Interactive charts and statistics
  - [Policy Briefs](/policy.html) — Summaries of key regulations and strategies

### Highlights (2026 Context)
- Projected record passenger traffic: **105.8 – 112.9 million** annually
- Aviation contribution to GDP: Significant growth from tourism and trade
- Visit Malaysia Year 2026 target: **35.6 million international arrivals**, RM147.1 billion in tourism receipts
- Focus areas: Sustainability, regional hub status, fleet expansion
